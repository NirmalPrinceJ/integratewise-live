# IntegrateWise User Documentation (External)

## 📖 Welcome to IntegrateWise

IntegrateWise is your proactive intelligence partner for Sales, CS, and Revenue operations.

### 🏁 Getting Started

1. **Connect Your Tools**: Go to Settings > Integrations to link Stripe, Salesforce, or ZenDesk.
2. **Monitor the Today View**: See high-priority Situations as they arise.
3. **Review & Approve**: Every automated action requires your sign-off by default (Human-in-the-loop).

### 🛠️ Common Workflows

#### 1. Billing Oversight (Revenue OS)

- **Problem**: Payment fails and customers are locked out too quickly.
- **Solution**: Use the **Billing Leniency** module to automatically apply a 7-day grace period upon the first failure.

#### 2. Lead Response (Sales OS)

- **Problem**: High-intent leads go cold because outreach takes hours.
- **Solution**: The **Lead Velocity** module triggers a Slack alert and CRM task within 60 seconds of a "Hot Lead" event.

### ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why am I seeing a specific Situation?**
A: Every Situation includes a "Why it matters" section and links to the specific "Evidence" (events/signals) that triggered it.

**Q: Can I automate actions?**
A: Yes. You can change the "Autonomy Level" from `manual` to `semi_auto` or `auto` for specific trusted workflows.
